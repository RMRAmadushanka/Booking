================================================================================
KEY INSIGHTS
================================================================================
✅ Use MUI For:
   • Dialogs/Modals (MenuBar, PackageFilters mobile)
   • Dropdowns/Menus (AvatarDropdown, LangDropdown, NotifyDropdown)
   • Date Pickers (All date range inputs)
   • Complex Forms (LocationInput → Autocomplete)
   • Navigation (NavMobile → Accordion)
   • Tabs (HeroSearchForm)

❌ Keep Tailwind For:
   • Layout components (Header, NavSection, Pages)
   • Brand components (Logo, Avatar, Cards)
   • Form wrappers (SearchForm layouts)
   • Simple buttons (ButtonClose, ClearDataButton)

⚠️ Consider MUI For:
   • Simple form controls (if consistency priority)
   • Switches (if accessibility critical)
